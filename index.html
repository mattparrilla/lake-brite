The above is a prototype of an in-progress visualization on Lake Champlain. Each image is an animated GIF of readings from the 15 monitoring stations of the Long Term Lake Monitoring Program. The program has been gathering data continuously since 1992.

Each frame represents a month of data. The values have been interpolated throughout the lake from the 15 stations. The GIFs are in sync and looping through years 1992 through 2014. At 2 frames per second you can see the entire data set in just over 2 minutes!

Please excuse the lack of visual context (such as a legend). As mentioned, it's a work in progress!
